THE MAGIC BOTTLE

https://magic.estudionk.com

DESIGN

Very nice design, super interactive, makes you want to learn more about the product.

USABILITY

Seems easy at first but after clicking “explore” becomes hard to navigate. It tries to showcase the bottle features but it’s hard to understand how to see the next one. Still not sure how to do it. 

CONTENT

Content is relevant, wish it would’ve been easier to access it all with a better usability. 

CREATIVITY

Nice approach to showcase a product. Daring touch to use “dark mode”.
