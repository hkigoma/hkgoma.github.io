TAMBIEN STUDIO

https://www.tambien.studio

DESIGN

Lots of movement, typography is nice but could have a better hierarchy.

USABILITY

A bit hard to understand at first, but you quickly learn how to navigate. Would probably be too hard for someone older to use.

CONTENT

The content is relevant but could be placed better or separated in different pages instead of a constant scrolling to access certain info.

CREATIVITY

Original design, not something you usually see in other websites but is similar to other design company.
